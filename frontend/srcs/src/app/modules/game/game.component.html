Pong

Look for an opponent

Game Description

You'll get assigned to either Team Blue or Team Red.
Your goal is to get the ball past the other player's paddle.
At the end of the match, the player who scored the most goals wins.
To avoid getting scored, bounce the ball with your paddle.

Game Controls

Use the up and down keys on your keyboard to move the paddle in that direction.

Game modes

Classic Game

Regular game. The ball has a constant speed and the game ends after 2 minutes (+ animation time).

Modded Game

Classic game, but Power-Ups may appear every now and then.
They can be activated by touching them with the ball and will have different effects:

Super Speed
The ball will gain a lot of speed for a short time.

Invisibility
The ball will turn invisible intermittently.

Shrinkage
The other player's paddle will become shorter but will recover over time.
